7th November 2023

"Proto-Expo," served as the grand culmination of the internship. This event was strategically scheduled to coincide with the celebration of National Entrepreneurship Day. Each team set up and displayed their prototypes, creating a dynamic exhibition space. School students, as well as a panel of jury members, were invited to witness the innovative solutions crafted by the interns.
