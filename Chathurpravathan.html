Success Story of Chathurpravarthan Private Limited

Chathur Pravarthan Private Limited,

established in 2022,

is a remarkable success story. It originated as a start-up within the premises of Malnad College of Engineering, born out of the successful implementation of the "Solar Panel Cleaning Robot" project. This project was made possible through the support of the New Age Innovation Network (NAIN) and K-tech, a Government of Karnataka initiative. NAIN provided funding for a total of nine projects, and Chathur Pravarthan's "Solar Panel Cleaning Robot" was one of the eight projects that achieved remarkable success. Led by Mr. Achal J and his team, with the mentorship ofDr. Geetha Kiran A and Dr. Mohana Lakshmi J, the project was successfully implemented and demonstrated. Following NAIN's guidance to promote start-ups emerging from the implemented projects, the team behind the

"Solar Panel Cleaning Robot"

took the initiative to establish Chathur Pravarthan Private Limited in November 2022, The company's directors include

Dr. Geetha Kiran A, Dr. Mohana Lakshmi J and Mr. Achal J.

Chathur Pravarthan Private Limited is now poised to revolutionize the market with its sophisticated and market-ready "Automated Solar Power Cleaning Robot". The company envisions providing a more advanced solution for the efficient cleaning of solar panels, catering to the growing demand in the renewable energy sector.
