About the Event

Build solutions for the future of Agri-Tech

Are you ready to innovate and build solutions for the future of Agri-Tech? 🌱💡 Join KrishiManthan — a 36-hour state level hackathon where students collaborate, innovate, and solve real-world agricultural challenges.

Participants can choose from curated problem statements or propose their own innovative agri-tech ideas. The best idea will receive incubation support, mentorship from CXOs, industry experts, and farmers — plus a Live Krishi Drone Show!
