Innovate & Elevate – NAIN 2.0 Student Project Evaluation at NHI

3rd October, 2024

The event was organized under the New Age Innovation Network 2.0 (NAIN 2.0), a flagship initiative by the Department of Electronics, IT, BT, Science & Technology, Government of Karnataka. A total of 26 student innovations were evaluated by a distinguished jury panel comprising experts from both industry and academia. As part of the NAIN 2.0 scheme, up to Rs. 5 lakh in funding will be provided for the top 10 promising projects, offering support to help bring these innovations to life.
