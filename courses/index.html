About the Program

The Micro Engineering Certification Program is intended to predominantly enhance skill sets in specific technical areas and provide significant industry-recognized credentials.

This certification program is jointly offered by the ME-RIISE FOUNDATION and ME-RIISE (Malnad Enclave for Research, Innovation, Incubation, Startups and Entrepreneurship), Malnad College of Engineering, Hassan, in collaboration with reputed academic institutions and industries.

The program focuses on practical learning, industry exposure, innovation, entrepreneurship, and employability enhancement through specialized certification courses.
