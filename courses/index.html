Micro-Engineering Certification Program

Empowering learners with industry-ready engineering and technology certification

Delivered by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering in partnership with ME-RIISE, Malnad College of Engineering, Hassan.

The Micro Engineering Certification Program is intended to predominantly enhance skill set in a specific technical area and receive significant credentials. This certification program is jointly offered by Department of Computer Science and Engineering and ME-RIISE (Malnad Enclave for Research, Innovation, Incubation, Startups and Entrepreneurship) , Malnad College of Engineering, Hassan in collaboration with reputed Academic Institutes and Industries. More details

Program objectives

  • Advance expertise in strategic technical areas with credentialed learning.
  • Gain skills for emerging fields in computer science and allied engineering.

The program welcomes engineering, diploma, and degree students, faculty members, job seekers, and professionals seeking career growth.

Program structure

Mode of delivery: Online / Offline

Duration: Core Java – 120 hours; other courses – 2 months (3–4 hrs/week).

Certification: Complete the course modules, assessments, and evaluations to receive the credential.

Course delivery & Certification

These courses include offline lectures with hands-on, online video lectures, quizzes, programming assignments and end of course examination. Learners are required to complete all the modules specified in the respective course and achieve minimum grade. Minimum grade will be specified by the institution.
