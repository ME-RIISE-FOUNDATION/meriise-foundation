Rajiv Gandhi Entrepreneurship Program (RGEP)

RGEP is an early-stage funding program that nurtures ideas and innovations to promote innovative endeavors, startups initiated by students or entrepreneurs.

Solar Power Solutions

Implementing cutting-edge solar technologies to provide reliable energy sources for rural households and businesses.

Wind Energy Initiatives

Harnessing wind power to create sustainable energy systems that reduce dependence on fossil fuels.

Community Education

Empowering locals with knowledge and skills to maintain and operate green energy systems independently.

Economic Development

Creating job opportunities and boosting local economies through green energy entrepreneurship.

Environmental Sustainability

Reducing carbon footprint and promoting eco-friendly practices for a greener future.

Innovative Technologies

Exploring and implementing the latest green energy technologies tailored for rural settings.

Join RGEP

Be part of the change. Support sustainable energy initiatives in rural communities.
