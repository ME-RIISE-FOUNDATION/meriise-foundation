OUR ACHIEVEMENTS

Additional Funding for RCEP

We are pleased to announce that MCE has received additional funds of 6,65,000 under Rajiv Gandhi Entrepreneurship Programme by Government of Karnataka raising the total grant amount to ₹19,95,000/- Congratulations to Dr. Geetha Kiran A, SPOC, PMP - RGEP, MCE. Special appreciation to mentors Dr. Geetha Kiran A, Dr. Nanditha B R, and Dr. Mohana Lakshmi J for their dedication in fostering innovation and entrepreneurship at MCE.

Inspired by the #ELEVATE 2024 Recognition

Truly a momentous occasion for the team "Chathur Pravarthan Private Limited", Dr. Geetha Kiran A, Dr. Mohana lakshmi J to be recognized under #ELEVATE2024 and awarded by Shri. Priyank Kharge, Honorable Minister of IT, BT, and S&T. This recognition is a testament to their relentless pursuit of innovation and entrepreneurship.

MCE is recognized as Program Monitoring Partner (PMP) Under RGEP by the Government of Karnataka.

We are pleased to announce that MCE has been recognized as a Program Monitoring Partner (PMP) under the Rajiv Gandhi Entrepreneurship Programme by the Government of Karnataka. Congratulations to Dr. Geetha Kiran A, SPOC, PMP - RGEP, MCE, for securing ₹13,30,000 in funding under this programme. Special appreciation to mentors Dr. Geetha Kiran A, Dr. Nanditha B R, and Dr. Mohana Lakshmi J for their dedication in fostering innovation and entrepreneurship at MCE.

Chathur Pravarthan Pvt. Ltd. Wins ₹21 Lakh Grant Under ELEVATE 2024!

Proud to announce that Chathur Pravarthan Private Limited, a startup incubated at Malnad College of Engineering, Hassan under ME-RIISE FOUNDATION, has been selected under ELEVATE 2024! Heartiest congratulations to the founders Dr. Geetha Kiran A, Dr. Mohana Lakshmi J, and Mr. Achal J for this remarkable achievement. The startup has been awarded a grant-in-aid of ₹21 lakhs by the Government of Karnataka. This milestone not only reinforces the strong startup ecosystem at MCE but also inspires students to pursue groundbreaking ideas.

The institute has been rated 4 stars out of 5 over the last 2 years

The Institution Innovation Council of Malnad College of Engineering has consistently performed well, earning a 4-star rating for the past two years and ranking in the 101-150 band in the NIRF Innovation Category 2023. As a result, the institute has been selected as a Mentor Institute under the Ministry of Education's Mentor-Mentee Scheme 2024, guiding five other institutions: Bangalore Institute of Technology, Channabasaveshwara Institute of Technology, Lal Bahadur Shastri College of Engineering, and the Maharaja Institutes of Technology (Mysore and Thandavapura). Key activities include orientation sessions, design thinking workshops, quarterly progress reviews, and feedback visits.

India's Best Engineering Institutions

Malnad College of Engineering has been recognized in the latest edition of Careers360’s annual assessment 'India's Best Engineering Institutions' 2024 issue. Our institution has been rated ‘AAA’ making it one of the best engineering institutes in the country in 2024. Institutions are evaluated and rated based on a comprehensive assessment of their academic performance, faculty quality, infrastructure, research output and student satisfaction.
