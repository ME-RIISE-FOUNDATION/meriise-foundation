Eminent Engineer Award 2024

Dr. Geetha Kiran A, CEO, ME-RIISE FOUNDATION, has been honored with the Eminent Engineer Award! by Institution of Engineers, Karnataka State Chapter, Bengaluru. Her outstanding leadership and relentless pursuit of innovation continue to uplift the engineering community. A true inspiration, her contributions pave the way for future engineers and leaders.

ME-RIISE FOUNDATION signed an MoU with SUPRMENTR

ME-RIISE FOUNDATION signed an MoU with SUPRMENTR, a Peer to Peer Career Mentorship Platform that helps students network with industry mentors, upskill, learn and prepare for Interviews.

We’re excited to announce that our event, "Empowering Youth through Community Centric Education," has been highlighted in local newspapers including Ecchara Vani, Hassan Mitra, Hassan Vani, Jana Hitha, and Hoysala Patha.This coverage underscores the positive impact of our efforts in fostering self-employment and innovation among the youth.

India's Best Engineering Institutions

Malnad College of Engineering has been recognized in the latest edition of Careers360’s annual assessment 'India's Best Engineering Institutions' 2024 issue. Our institution has been rated ‘AAA’ making it one of the best engineering institutes in the country in 2024. Institutions are evaluated and rated based on a comprehensive assessment of their academic performance, faculty quality, infrastructure, research output and student satisfaction.

The institute has been rated 4 stars out of 5 over the last 2 years

The Institution Innovation Council of Malnad College of Engineering has consistently performed well, earning a 4-star rating for the past two years and ranking in the 101-150 band in the NIRF Innovation Category 2022. As a result, the institute has been selected as a Mentor Institute under the Ministry of Education's Mentor-Mentee Scheme 2023, guiding five other institutions: Bangalore Institute of Technology, Channabasaveshwara Institute of Technology, Lal Bahadur Shastri College of Engineering, and the Maharaja Institutes of Technology (Mysore and Thandavapura). Key activities include orientation sessions, design thinking workshops, quarterly progress reviews, and feedback visits.

Mentor in the Ministry of Education's Mentor-Mentee scheme 2023

Excited to announce that Malnad College of Engineering has been chosen as a Mentor in the Ministry of Education's Mentor-Mentee scheme 2023! This feat has been achieved by consistently maintaining 4 star rating in IIC and getting placed in the rank band of 101-150 in NIRF INNOVATION. With the support of Dr. Geetha Kiran A, President, IIC & CEO, ME-RIISE FOUNDATION, we look forward to nurturing innovation and fostering a dynamic ecosystem across 5 institutes. Let's embark on this journey of innovation together
